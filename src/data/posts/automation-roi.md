Many businesses hesitate to automate because of the upfront development cost. However, they often fail to calculate the "Cost of Inaction."

### calculating the Time-Save
If a mid-level employee spends 4 hours a week on data entry, that is roughly 200 hours a year.
* **Manual Cost:** $50/hr x 200 hrs = $10,000 per year, per employee.
* **Mashwerx Automation Cost:** One-time build of $5,000.

The ROI is realized in 6 months. After that, it is pure profit.
